    Impulse


  • It is a term that evaluates the overall effect of a force acting over time.
  • It is given the Symbol J.
  • It is expressed in Newton-Seconds
  • To get the Impulse, we multiply force with time.
  • J = Ft

    Valence Electrons


  • They are located in the outermost electron shell of an atom.
  • The ourermost electron shells are called valence shells, therefore these electrongs are called Valence Electrons
  • They are the furthest from the nucleus, therefore being the least tightly held by the atom
  • The number of valences electrongs determines the reactivity, electronegativity, and the number of bonds it can form.
  • You can use the periodic table to help you determine the amount of valence electrongs in an element using the group number.
